H-Net: Humanities & Social Sciences Online
H-Net: Humanities and Social Sciences Online is an independent, non-profit scholarly association that offers an open academic space for scholars, teachers, advanced students and related professionals. Our vision is to see the humanities and social sciences transformed by the immense potential of digital technologies oriented around moderated intellectual exchange, collaborative production, and the open dissemination of knowledge.

H-Net's work is guided and informed by our commitment to: high standards of scholarly, pedegogical and professional behavior; a pluralistic and democratic way of decision-making; and the principles of Open Access.
