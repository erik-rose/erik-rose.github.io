Humanities & Social Sciences Online
About

Vision

Our vision is of the humanities and social sciences transformed by the immense potential of digital technologies and oriented around moderated intellectual exchange, collaborative production, and the open dissemination of knowledge.


Mission

H-Net: Humanities and Social Sciences Online (H-Net) is an independent, non-profit scholarly association that offers an open academic space for scholars, teachers, advanced students and related professionals. Built around an online system of networks moderated by certified editors, H-Net is uniquely situated to encourage technological innovation in the humanities and social sciences while safeguarding academic best practices.


Values

H-Net's work is guided and informed by our commitment to:

  • high standards of scholarly, pedagogical and professional behavior
  • a pluralistic and democratic way of decision-making
  • principles of Open Access
